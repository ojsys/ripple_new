Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I start a project on StartUpRipple?
Simply create an account as a founder, then click "Create Project" from your dashboard. Fill in your project details, set your funding goal, and submit for approval. Our team typically reviews projects within 2-3 business days.
What are SRT tokens and how do they work?
SRT (StartUp Ripple Tokens) are our unique investment mechanism that allows partners to invest in a diversified portfolio of vetted African startups. Purchase tokens, allocate them to ventures, and earn returns as those ventures succeed.
How does the accelerator program work?
Our 12-week accelerator program provides intensive mentorship, up to $50,000 in seed funding, and access to our network of investors and partners. Apply through the Programs section and our team will review your application.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept payments through Paystack, which supports bank transfers, card payments (Visa, Mastercard), and mobile money across multiple African countries. All transactions are secure and encrypted.
How can I track my investments?
Log into your dashboard to see real-time updates on all your investments. You'll receive regular updates from project founders and can track funding progress, milestones, and returns directly from your account.
